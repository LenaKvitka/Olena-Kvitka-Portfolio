Author's portrait with your unique story

Dear friends! You can see an example of my work "Children in Babylon", 2022. The portrait on canvas was made in the technique of oil painting with the addition of the idea of ​​the Tower of Babel, as a symbol of the crisis of humanity and an expression of the language barrier. This painting expresses a personal story. You can also order a portrait from a photo for yourself or your loved ones, taking into account your personal vision and history. Please feel free to contact me with questions and wishes by e-mail. Sincerely, artist Olena Kvitka.
